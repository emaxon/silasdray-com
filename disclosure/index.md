<p><strong>This site earns no affiliate revenue and accepts no advertising.</strong></p>

<p>silasdray.com exists to host the public-facing presence of Silas Dray's serialized novel <em>Ninety-Four</em> and its sequels. The site does not contain affiliate links, sponsored content, or paid product placements. The author does not accept compensation in exchange for editorial content, recommendations, or endorsements on this site.</p>

<h2>Revenue from the work itself</h2>

<p>Future revenue from the books and podcast may include:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Sales of the eBook, paperback, and audiobook editions of <em>Ninety-Four</em> through their respective retail platforms (Amazon, Apple Books, Findaway, etc.).</li>
  <li>Direct sales of any future supplementary materials (special editions, signed copies, etc.) — none currently exist.</li>
  <li>Podcast sponsorships within the audio episodes, clearly disclosed as such if and when they occur.</li>
</ul>

<p>This site itself is not a revenue channel. It is a marketing and distribution surface for the work.</p>

<h2>Changes to this disclosure</h2>

<p>If this changes — for example, if the site begins to host affiliate links to the retail platforms where the book is sold — this disclosure will be updated and dated below.</p>
