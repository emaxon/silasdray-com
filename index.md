<h2 class="book-title">Ninety-Four</h2>
<p class="book-status">Coming soon</p>

<p class="lede">A government data analyst notices a number that should not be different.</p>

<p>Mara Salcedo has worked the same desk at the Social Security Administration for eleven years. She is good at her job because she remembers numbers the way other people remember faces. On a Tuesday morning in October, two snapshots of the same population mismatch by ninety-four people. By Wednesday, both systems show the same number. Identical. Down to the unit.</p>

<p>She tells nobody. She files the ticket. She goes home.</p>

<p>What follows is the record she keeps.</p>

<p><em>Ninety-Four</em> is the first book in a planned trilogy. A new chapter every week. Audio first &mdash; the chapters are also episodes. eBook and paperback at season's end.</p>
