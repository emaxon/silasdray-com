  <h1>NINETY-FOUR</h1>
  <p class="lede">A serialized novel. Thirty episodes.</p>

  <h2>THE PREMISE</h2>

  <p>Mara Salcedo has spent fifteen years at the Social Security Administration's program service center in Kansas City doing post-payment integrity work. She notices when numbers don't match. In October, she notices ninety-four people present in a validation dataset who are not present in the same dataset the following day. The discrepancy resolves correctly. The audit trail is clean. The export she ran herself, on her own credentials, tells a different story.</p>

  <p><em>Ninety-Four</em> is the first book in a planned series of three. Mara does not solve it in this book.</p>
