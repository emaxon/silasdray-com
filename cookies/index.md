<p><strong>This site uses cookies for analytics. No advertising cookies. No third-party tracking beyond analytics.</strong></p>

<h2>What cookies are used</h2>

<p>Once analytics are provisioned (see below), the site uses Google Analytics 4 to measure aggregate traffic patterns. The relevant cookies are:</p>

<ul>
  <li><span class="mono">_ga</span> — distinguishes unique visitors. Duration: 2 years.</li>
  <li><span class="mono">_ga_&lt;container&gt;</span> — session-state for Google Analytics 4. Duration: 2 years.</li>
</ul>

<p>These cookies do not contain personally identifying information.</p>

<h2>What cookies are not used</h2>

<ul>
  <li>No advertising cookies.</li>
  <li>No retargeting pixels (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.).</li>
  <li>No third-party tracking beyond Google Analytics.</li>
  <li>No fingerprinting or non-cookie identifiers.</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to opt out</h2>

<p>You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Doing so does not affect site functionality. You can also install Google's <a href="https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout">Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on</a> to specifically opt out of GA4.</p>
