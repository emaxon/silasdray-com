<p><strong>This site collects one thing: your email address, if you choose to provide it.</strong></p>

<p>That email is stored by SendFox, an email service provider, and is used solely to notify you when new episodes of <em>Ninety-Four</em> are released. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send. When you unsubscribe, your address is removed from active sending lists.</p>

<h2>What we don't collect</h2>

<ul>
  <li>We do not collect your name unless you provide it in correspondence.</li>
  <li>We do not collect payment information; the site does not currently sell anything.</li>
  <li>We do not sell, trade, or share your email address with anyone outside the operations of this site.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Analytics</h2>

<p>This site uses analytics to understand aggregate traffic patterns (which pages are read, where readers come from). Analytics data is anonymized and does not identify individual visitors. See our <a href="{{ '/cookies/' | relative_url }}">Cookies</a> page for details.</p>

<h2>Data retention</h2>

<p>Email addresses are retained as long as you remain subscribed. After you unsubscribe, your address is removed from active sending lists within 30 days; backup retention by SendFox follows their own policy.</p>

<h2>Your rights</h2>

<p>You can request access to or deletion of your subscription data by emailing <span class="mono">privacy@silasdray.com</span>. (Note: this contact channel is monitored on a best-effort basis.)</p>

<h2>Changes to this policy</h2>

<p>If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with the date below. Material changes will be communicated to subscribers in a regular email.</p>
